Who are we?

We are two passionate foodies, Rose Hewetson and Scotch Macdonald,

who wish to share our love for cooking delicious feasts.

We make sure we only use the most sustainably sourced

produce we can get our hands on.

What do we do?

We host pop up culinary events in fun locations, which focus

strongly on choosing the most flavourful and delicious seasonal produce.

Our events sit usually between 15 - 35 guests and are open to anyone who wants to dine with us.
