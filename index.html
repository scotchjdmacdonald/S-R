Who are we?

We are two passionate foodies, Rose Hewetson and Scotch Macdonald,

who are all about sharing our love for cooking delicious feasts.

Making sure we only use the most sustainably sourced produce

and ingredients, we can get our hands on.

What do we do?

We host pop up culinary events in always changing and interesting locations.

Focusing strongly on choosing the most flavourful and delicious in season produce.

Our events sit usually between 15 - 35 guests and are open to anyone who wants to dine with us.
